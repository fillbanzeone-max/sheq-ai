Risk Consequence Thresholds
Level Health & Safety Environmental Legal / Compliance Quality
I1 – Insignificant Report & assessment only. No injury. No impact. Contained within operations area. Prompt cleanup within shift. Single admin exceedance (e.g. late data submission). No regulatory response. Out-of-spec production <1 hr. Minor re-processing.
I2 – Minor First Aid Injury (FAC). Minor impact contained on site. Cleanup within 1 week. Repeat low-impact exceedance. Low-level admin regulatory response. Report within 1 month. Out-of-spec <8 hrs. Return of shipment or sales credit.
I3 – Moderate Medical Treatment Injury (MTC) or Restricted Work Injury (RWI). Moderate impact within lease boundary. Medium-term cleanup (<1 month). Multiple repeat exceedances. Prosecution possible. Notify regulator within 48 hrs. Out-of-spec for 1 week. Loss of minor customer / order cancellation.
I4 – Significant Single Lost Time Injury (LTI) or Permanent Disability. OEL exceedance without PPE. Significant impact extending beyond lease boundary. Significant external cleanup. Recurring exceedances. Prosecution & penalties likely. Threat to license to operate. Out-of-spec 1 month. Loss of major customer. Significant reprocessing.
I5 – Critical Multiple LTIs, Fatality(ies), or serious chronic long-term health effects. PEL/STEL exceedance. Permanent & widespread severe impact to land, water or air. Government suspension of licence. Criminal investigation, jail terms. Licences revoked. Major sustained sanctions. Out-of-spec >1 month. Critical utilisation loss. Loss of market confidence.
